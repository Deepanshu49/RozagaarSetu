RozgaarSetu

Rozgar Setu is a scheme launched by the Madhya Pradesh government to help skilled workers who returned home due to the COVID-19 pandemic find employment. The initiative acts as a bridge between workers and employers, ensuring industries get the manpower they need while workers secure jobs. Under this scheme, the government identifies skilled workers who previously worked in industries and connects them with employers looking for labor. The workers are categorized based on their skills, such as construction, mining, textiles, agriculture, and government sectors. Employers can then hire workers through a common portal, making the process efficient.
